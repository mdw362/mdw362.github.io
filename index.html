About Me

I am a computer scientist at the US Department of Defense and also work with Srinivas Narayana from Rutgers University in network hardware offload. I am broadly interested in computer systems research and am especially drawn towards networks, computer architecture, programming languages, and program analysis. I graduated from New York University in 2019 with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematics where I was advised by Anirudh Sivaraman in my work with programmable switches.
